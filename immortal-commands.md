### Goto
---
Syntax: `goto <vnum|area|mob|player>` - Teleports you to the selected location.

### Goxy
---
Syntax: `goxy <x> <y> [map id]` - Teleports you to the selected coordinates. If you are on a wilderness map already, map id may be omitted to teleport on current map.
